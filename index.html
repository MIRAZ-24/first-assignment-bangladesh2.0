The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 envisions a transformative future by leveraging advanced technologies, fostering inclusive economic growth, and prioritizing sustainability. Through strategic initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and resilient infrastructure development, Bangladesh is poised to establish itself as a global benchmark for progress and innovation.

Digital Transformation

Establishing a comprehensive digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and ensure equitable access to services for all citizens nationwide.

Green Energy

Driving investments in solar and wind energy to foster a sustainable and environmentally friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Developing state-of-the-art infrastructure to drive economic growth and enhance global competitiveness.

Within each of us lies an untapped entrepreneurial spirit waiting to be awakened. Poverty persists not due to laziness or inability but because of limited opportunities, inequitable systems, and uneven resource distribution. By equipping the underserved with access to credit and tools for self-reliance, we unlock their true potential. Prioritizing people over profit paves the way for a world where everyone has the opportunity to prosper.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
